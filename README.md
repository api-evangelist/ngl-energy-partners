# NGL Energy Partners (ngl-energy-partners)

NGL Energy Partners LP is a diversified midstream master limited partnership providing transportation, storage, blending, and marketing services for crude oil, natural gas liquids, refined products, and water solutions to producers and end-users across the energy industry. The company is publicly traded on the NYSE under the ticker symbol NGL and operates three primary business units: Crude Oil Logistics, Water Solutions, and NGL Liquids.
